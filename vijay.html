# Article About Vijay Vijay, also known as “Thalapathy,” is one of the most famous actors in Tamil cinema. His full name is Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar. He was born on June 22, 1974, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. His father, S. A. Chandrasekar, is a film director, and his mother, Shoba Chandrasekar, is a singer. ([Encyclopedia Britannica][1]) Vijay started acting as a child artist in Tamil movies. Later, he became a lead actor with the film *Naalaiya Theerpu* in 1992. After that, he acted in many successful films such as *Poove Unakkaga*, *Ghilli*, *Pokkiri*, *Thuppakki*, *Mersal*, *Master*, and *Leo*. His movies are popular for action, dance, comedy, and emotional scenes. ([Encyclopedia Britannica][1]) He is loved by millions of fans for his simple personality and helping nature. Vijay is also famous for his dancing skills and powerful dialogues. Because of his huge fan following, he became one of the highest-paid actors in South India. ([Wikipedia][2]) In 2024, Vijay entered politics and started his political party called Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK). He said he wants to work for the people and bring positive changes to society. ([Encyclopedia Britannica][1]) Vijay has received many awards for his acting career. Today, he is not only a cinema star but also an inspiration to many young people. His hard work, dedication, and success made him one of the biggest stars in India. ([Wikipedia][2])
